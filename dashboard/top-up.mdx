Flatkey uses a prepaid balance model — you add credit to your account and it is consumed as your API requests use models. There are no subscriptions or recurring charges. Every top-up earns bonus credit, credited instantly to your balance.

## Top-up tiers

| Top-up | Bonus | Total credit | Effective saving |
|---|---|---|---|
| $10 | +$3 | $13 | 30% |
| $20 | +$8 | $28 | 40% |
| $200 | +$100 | $300 | **50% (best value)** |

<Tip>
  The $200 top-up tier offers the best effective rate. Combined with model discounts (60–90% of official list), this brings costs to as low as 50% of official provider pricing.
</Tip>

## Adding a top-up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Balance">
    In the [console](https://console.flatkey.ai), click **Balance** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a top-up amount">
    Choose $10, $20, or $200. The bonus credit is displayed before you confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    Enter your card details and confirm. Payment is processed via Stripe. The credit (including bonus) is added to your balance immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How bonus credit works

- **Instant credit**: Bonus credit appears in your balance as soon as payment is processed.
- **Never expires**: Bonus credit and paid credit both remain in your balance indefinitely — there is no expiry date.
- **Spends like normal credit**: Bonus credit is consumed the same way as paid credit. There is no restriction on which models it can be used for.
- **Stacks every time**: Each top-up earns a new bonus. Top up $200 three times and earn $300 in bonus credit total.

## Balance management

Your balance is displayed in the **Balance** section of the console. You can:

- View the breakdown between paid credit and bonus credit
- See the history of all top-up transactions with dates and amounts
- Enable low-balance email alerts from **Settings → Notifications**

## What happens when balance runs out

If your balance drops to $0.00, API requests will return a `401 Insufficient Balance` error until you top up. There are no automatic charges or overdraft — your requests simply stop until you add more credit.

<Warning>
  For production workloads, set up a low-balance alert to ensure you top up before the balance is fully consumed. Go to **Settings → Notifications** to configure the threshold.
</Warning>
